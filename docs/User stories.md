# User Stories

## Patients

### 1. Account Creation & Update || Priority: High || Estimate: 3 days
**Description**
As a patient, I want to have an account with my personal details so I won't need to manually enter them every time I book an appointment. In addition, I want to view & edit the information easily at any time.

___

### 2. Login to System || Priority: High || Estimate: 2 days
**Description**
As a registered patient, I want to log into the system so that I can manage my appointments.

___

### 3. Search for Doctors || Priority: High || Estimate: 3 days
**Description**
As a patient, I want to search for doctors by specialty or name so that I can find the right healthcare professional. I want to view their information before I book.

___

### 4. View Doctor Availability || Priority: High || Estimate: 4 days
**Description**
As a patient, I want to view available appointment slots so that I can choose a convenient time.

___

### 5. Book Appointment || Priority: High || Estimate: 6 days
**Description**
As a patient, I want to book an appointment with a doctor so that I can receive medical consultation.

___

### 6. Receive Appointment Confirmation || Priority: Medium || Estimate: 2 days
**Description**
As a patient, I want to receive confirmation after booking so that I know my appointment is scheduled.

___

### 7. View Upcoming Appointments || Priority: High || Estimate: 2 days
**Description**
As a patient, I want to see my upcoming appointments so that I can keep track of my schedule.

___

### 8. Cancel Appointment || Priority: Medium || Estimate: 2 days
**Description**
As a patient, I want to cancel an appointment if I cannot attend.

___

### 9. Reschedule Appointment || Priority: Medium || Estimate: 2 days
**Description**
As a patient, I want to reschedule my appointment so that I can choose a different time.

____

### 10. Receive Appointment Reminder || Priority: Medium || Estimate: 3 days
**Description**
As a patient, I want to receive reminders so that I do not forget my appointment.


## Admin

### 11. Add Doctor Profile || Priority: High || Estimate: 5 days
**Description**
As an administrator, I want to add doctor profiles so that patients can check for their information before booking.

___

### 12. Add Doctor Schedules || Priority: High || Estimate: 5 days
**Description**
As an administrator, I want to add doctor availability so that patients can book appointments with them.

___

### 13. Administrate currently booked appointments || Priority: High || Estimate: 4 days
**Description**
As an administrator, I want to view and update booked appointments and see current appointment status.

___

### 14. Manage Users || Priority: High || Estimate: 3 days
**Description**
As an administrator, I want to manage user accounts so that I can maintain system security.

___

### 15. Have security alerts || Priority: Low || Estimate: 2 days
**Description**
As an administrator, I want to know who is logged into the system and if there are any suspicious activities.

___

### 16. Manage room availablity || Priority: Medium || Estimate: 4 days
**Description**
As an administrator, I want to manage room availability so that hospital resources are efficiently used.

___
